ABOUT ME

Reconnecting with Natural Movement

I bring a unique perspective to health and wellness, shaped by a career journey that has taken me through molecular biology, software engineering, and now into fitness and nutrition coaching. Along the way, I've lived and worked in countries across Europe, Africa, Asia, the Middle East, and North America—experiences that have given me a deep appreciation for different cultures, lifestyles, and ways of approaching health.

This diverse background taught me to combine scientific curiosity with a practical, real-world approach. Whether in the lab, the tech world, or the gym, I've always been driven by a desire to understand how things work and to help people apply that knowledge to improve their lives. Today, that means supporting clients in building healthier habits, feeling stronger in their bodies, and finding approaches to fitness and nutrition that truly fit their lives.

I help people reconnect with their bodies through a gentle, personalized approach to movement and fitness. My training blends mindful movement practices with modern functional exercise, creating a space where strength, flexibility, and ease naturally develop.

I believe fitness should feel supportive—not intimidating. My programs focus on bodyweight movement, animal-inspired flows, and mindful energy work to help you build strength and mobility at your own pace, without the pressure of extreme workouts or unrealistic expectations.
