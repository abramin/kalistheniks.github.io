ABOUT ME

Reconnecting with Natural Movement

I help people reconnect with their bodies through a gentle, personalized approach to movement and fitness. My training blends mindful movement practices with modern functional exercise, creating a space where strength, flexibility, and ease naturally develop.

I believe fitness should feel supportive—not intimidating. My programs focus on bodyweight movement, animal-inspired flows, and mindful energy work to help you build strength and mobility at your own pace, without the pressure of extreme workouts or unrealistic expectations.

TRAINING METHODS

Animal Movement

Reconnect with movement patterns inspired by animals. Improve mobility, coordination, and functional strength through ground-based movements.

Calisthenics

Master your bodyweight through progressive calisthenics training. Develop strength, control, and athleticism using minimal equipment.

Qigong

Cultivate energy and mindfulness through ancient Chinese movement practices. Improve breathing, posture, and mind-body connection.

Traditional Training

Strength and conditioning through weights and cardio. Customized programs to build muscle, burn fat, and improve cardiovascular health.
